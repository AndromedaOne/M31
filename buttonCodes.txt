0 - Left Stick X (Horizontal) Axis
1 - Left Stick Y (Vertical) Axis
2 - Left Trigger (T/F)
3 - Right Trigger (T/F)
4 - Right Stick X (Horizontal) Axis
5 - Right Stick Y (Vertical) Axis

Buttons:

1 - A Button
2 - B Button
3 - X Button
4 - Y Button
5 - Left Button
6 - Right Button
7 - Back Button
